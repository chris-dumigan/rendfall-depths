IMPORTANT FORMATTING REQUIREMENTS
- The monster must be centered consistently inside every 512 x 512 frame.
- Keep the monster scale consistent across all quadrants and directions.
- Do not crop weapons, limbs, hair, horns, shields, chains, projectiles, or effects.
- Leave enough padding around the monster so every animation fits cleanly.
- Use a high-contrast flat background color, not black if the monster has dark outlines.
- Do not include text, labels, frame numbers, grid lines, UI, shadows outside the character, or extra objects.
- Keep the style consistent with dark fantasy pixel-art action RPG sprites.
- The silhouette should be readable at small in-game size.
- The monster should face the exact direction for each row.
- The run, attack, block, and death animations should all feel physically connected to the same character design.
- Preserve the same pose scale and foot position as much as possible so the game collision footprint can remain stable.
