================================================================
  RENDFALL DEPTHS - CHARACTER SKILLS REFERENCE
================================================================
  Base CD  = raw cooldown before INT reduction.
  Start CD = cooldown with starting INT applied.
  Damage values are based on the hero's primary stat.
  Skill upgrades multiply current damage by 1.3, rounded.
  INT reduces cooldowns by 0.2s per point, minimum 0.5s.
================================================================


BARBARIAN
Primary stat: STR
Base stats: STR 10 / AGI 8 / INT 4
Starting HP: 100
Starting speed: 3.5

| Skill     | Key | Base CD | Start CD | Formula     | Lv1 | Lv2 | Lv3 | Max |
|-----------|-----|---------|----------|-------------|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| Slash     | Q   | 4.0s    | 3.2s     | 1x STR      | 10  | 13  | 17  | 3   |
| Whirlwind | W   | 8.0s    | 7.2s     | 1.5x STR    | 15  | 20  | 26  | 3   |
| Slam      | E   | 14.0s   | 13.2s    | 1.75x STR   | 18  | 23  | 30  | 3   |
| Berserk   | R   | 30.0s   | 29.2s    | buff        | -   | -   | -   | 2   |

Berserk:
  Lv1: 10s duration, 1.75x damage dealt, 1.5x damage taken, berserk sprites.
  Lv2: 10s duration, 2.0x damage dealt, 1.5x damage taken, berserk sprites.
  Berserk does not grow the model, range, or hitboxes.

Notes:
  Slash is a directional melee hit.
  Whirlwind is a circular AoE around the hero.
  Slam is a circular AoE, slows enemies, and grants brief damage immunity.


ROGUE
Primary stat: AGI
Base stats: STR 6 / AGI 10 / INT 6
Starting HP: 60
Starting speed: 3.75

| Skill        | Key | Base CD | Start CD | Formula       | Lv1 | Lv2 | Lv3 | Max |
|--------------|-----|---------|----------|---------------|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| Slash        | Q   | 4.0s    | 2.8s     | 1x AGI        | 10  | 13  | 17  | 3   |
| Throw        | W   | 6.0s    | 4.8s     | 1x AGI        | 10  | 13  | 17  | 3   |
| Wind Walk    | E   | 14.0s   | 12.8s    | bonus to Slash| 18  | 21  | 24  | 3   |
| Slice & Dice | R   | 30.0s   | 28.8s    | 3x AGI AoE    | 30  | 39  | -   | 2   |

Wind Walk:
  Doubles movement speed for 10s and makes enemies lose tracking.
  Entry animation plays on cast.
  The first damaging skill breaks Wind Walk and plays the exit animation.
  If Slash breaks Wind Walk, Slash gains bonus damage:
    Lv1: +1.75x primary stat
    Lv2: +2.05x primary stat
    Lv3: +2.35x primary stat

Notes:
  Throw fires a fast dagger in the direction the Rogue was facing when cast.
  Throw plays Knife Throw.mp3 on cast and Knife Throw Impact.wav on hit.
  Slice & Dice is a long-cooldown AoE ultimate around the Rogue.
  Slice & Dice plays Rogue Slash.wav 3 times, 0.15s apart.

  Blink Lv2 grants a second charge 1s after the first blink; cooldown starts after the second charge.


SHARED RULES
Stats:
  STR: +10 max HP per point, +5 current HP on allocation.
  AGI: +5% move speed per point.
  INT: -0.2s cooldown per point.

Primary stat:
  Damage scales from the currently selected primary stat.
  Plasticity lets the hero choose STR, AGI, or INT as primary.
  Plasticity also gives +3 bonus stat points per level going forward.
  The portrait border changes color to the selected primary stat.

Level-up:
  Level-up pauses gameplay.
  Skill selection shows current and upgraded scaling.
  Stat selection uses arrow keys.
  Talent selection opens a confirmation screen before applying.
