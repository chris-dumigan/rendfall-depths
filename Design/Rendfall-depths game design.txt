========================================================
  RENDFALL DEPTHS  —  Current Game Design Reference
  (Updated from original design doc to reflect implemented state)
========================================================


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
OVERVIEW
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Title:       Rendfall Depths
Genre:       Progressive Rogue-like Action RPG
Engine:      HTML5 Canvas 2D (single-file, no framework)
Stages:      10, increasing in difficulty
Death:       Game over, all progress lost
Win:         Clear all 10 stages

Dev Options (on death screen):
  [R]  Respawn at current stage with full HP
  [S]  Skip to next stage


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SCREENS & GAME STATES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  title       — Main menu (Game Art 1.png background, Cinzel Decorative font)
  menu        — Hero class selection
  playing     — Active gameplay
  levelup     — Level-up menu (multi-phase: skill → stats → talent)
  pendant     — Pendant drop notification (pauses game)
  stagechoice — Rest or Continue choice between stages
  stageclear  — Stage summary
  gameover    — Death screen


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
STATS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Strength     — +10 max HP per point
  Agility      — +5% movement speed per point
  Intelligence — −0.25s cooldown per point


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
HERO CLASSES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

BARBARIAN
  Primary stat:   Strength
  Base stats:     STR 10 / AGI 8 / INT 4
  Starting HP:    100
  Starting damage: 10

  Q  Slash        1× STR dmg         4s  CD   (basic melee swing)
  W  Whirlwind    1.5× STR AoE       8s  CD   (surrounds hero, hits all nearby)
  E  Slam         2× STR AoE         14s CD   (knockback slow 50% / 2s, 0.25s invul on cast)
  R  Berserk      —                  30s CD   (10s duration: 2× size, 2× attack range,
                                               2× damage dealt, 1.5× damage taken)


ROGUE
  Primary stat:   Agility
  Base stats:     STR 6 / AGI 10 / INT 6
  Starting HP:    60
  Starting damage: 10

  Q  Slash        1× AGI dmg         4s  CD   (quick melee strike)
  W  Throw        1.5× AGI dmg       6s  CD   (dagger projectile in facing direction)
  E  Wind Walk    —                  14s CD   (enemies lose tracking; sprite tinted teal;
                                               breaks on first damaging Slash for
                                               2x/2.3x/2.6x AGI bonus damage by level;
                                               exit animation plays)
  R  Slice & Dice —                  25s CD   (5s: all cooldowns tick at 2× speed)


MAGE
  Primary stat:   Intelligence
  Base stats:     STR 4 / AGI 6 / INT 12
  Starting HP:    60
  Starting damage: 10

  Q  Slash        1× INT dmg         4s  CD
  W  Fireball     1.5× INT dmg       6s  CD   (projectile in facing direction)
  E  Frost Nova   1× INT dmg         14s CD   (AoE stun 2s, 4× hero size radius)
  R  Blink        —                  5s  CD   (teleport 8× hero dimensions forward)


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
MOVEMENT & COMBAT
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Movement:     Arrow keys, normalized vector (smooth, no 8-dir snapping)
  Base speed:   ~3 units, modified by AGI (+5% per point) and talents
  Attack range: Scales with hero model size. Hitboxes are size-dependent per monster type.

  Attack range visualization (while attacking):
    AoE abilities  → semi-transparent circle overlay
    Directional    → dashed rectangle showing hit zone

  Damage numbers float upward from the hit point.
  XP numbers appear in purple above the damage number on enemy death.


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
LEVELING SYSTEM
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  XP to level:  100 flat per level (no scaling)
  XP decay:     base × 0.5^(aliveFrames / 2700)
                → each monster's XP halves every 45 seconds alive
                → minimum payout: 1 XP regardless of decay
                → incentivizes fast kills; shown live in the purple XP popup

  Level-up menu phases (in order per level-up):
    1. Skill upgrade  — choose one skill to level (Q / W / E, R unlocks at Lv6+)
    2. Stat points    — distribute 3 points (STR / AGI / INT); Plasticity grants +3 to all stats when chosen
    3. Talent select  — only at levels 3, 6, and 9

  Skill caps:   Q / W / E max level 3 · R max level 2
  Power gain:   +30% per skill level

  Level unlock table:
    Lv 1   Base stats, Q only
    Lv 2   Upgrade Q/W/E · 3 stat points
    Lv 3   Upgrade Q/W/E · 3 stat points · select talent (tier 1)
    Lv 4   Upgrade Q/W/E · 3 stat points
    Lv 5   Upgrade Q/W/E · 3 stat points
    Lv 6   Upgrade Q/W/E/R · 3 stat points · select talent (tier 2)
    Lv 7   Upgrade Q/W/E · 3 stat points
    Lv 8   Upgrade Q/W/E/R · 3 stat points
    Lv 9   Upgrade Q/W/E · 3 stat points · select talent (tier 3)
    Lv 10  Upgrade Q/W/E/R · 3 stat points


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TALENT TREE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  3 paths (Offensive / Defensive / Utility), each with 3 tiers.
  Tier 1 unlocks at Lv3, Tier 2 at Lv6, Tier 3 at Lv9.
  Higher tiers require the previous tier of the same path.

  OFFENSIVE (red)
    Tier 1  Haste              Cooldowns recover 15% faster
    Tier 2  Bloodlust          5% lifesteal; doubled briefly after kills
    Tier 3  Executioner        +25% damage vs enemies below 35% HP

  DEFENSIVE (blue)
    Tier 1  Stoneskin          15% less damage taken
    Tier 2  Lifeblood          Regen 1% max HP every 3 seconds when not surrounded
    Tier 3  Ancient Bulwark    +50% max HP; blocks the first hit each stage

  UTILITY (green)
    Tier 1  Plasticity         +3 to all stats immediately and choose primary attribute
    Tier 2  Assassin           Every 15s, next hit deals +1.5× primary stat damage
    Tier 3  Eternal Focus      Every fourth skill cast refunds 50% of that skill cooldown


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
BETWEEN STAGES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Players choose one of:
    Rest     — Heal to full HP, lose current-level XP progress
    Continue — Keep all XP, no healing

  Before choosing, players can review character stats, inventory (pendants),
  and skill/talent tooltips.


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PENDANTS  (mini-boss drops)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Pendants appear as a full-screen notification (game paused) when dropped.
  They are shown in the HUD as 68×68 icon slots on the right side of the bar.
  Hover over an icon to see the full tooltip with name and effect.
  Active pendant effects are listed under the stat block in the HUD.

  STAGE 3 — Minotaur drop table (100% drop, one of three at equal probability)
    Pendant of Vitality      +50 max HP
    Pendant of Swiftness     +20% movement speed
    Pendant of Acuity        All cooldowns −1 second

  STAGE 8 — Spell Blade drop table (100% drop, one of three at equal probability)
    Pendant of Might         +10 STR · +25% all skill range and AoE
    Pendant of the Assassin  +10 AGI · next damaging skill hit deals +15 bonus damage
                              (proc resets every 15 seconds)
    Pendant of Enlightenment +10 INT · absorbs one hit every 10 seconds


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
STAGE TABLE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

STAGE 1 — Goblin Swarm
  Count:   6 goblins
  Size:    70% of display tile
  HP:      10
  Damage:  10 per hit   ATK CD: 2s
  Behavior: Chase and melee-attack. Separation force prevents stacking.
  XP base: 14 per kill  (total up to 84 if killed fast)

STAGE 2 — Clay Golems
  Count:   3 golems
  Size:    120% of display tile
  HP:      150
  Behavior:
    — Always chase at GOLEM_SPEED (faster than basic ENEMY_SPEED)
    — AoE Slam: triggers within 2.2× display range
        · Windup: ~1.3s (expanding orange circle telegraph)
        · Damage: 32 (AoE in slam radius)
        · Slows hero 50% for 3 seconds
        · Cooldown: ~2.7s
    — Enrage at 40% HP: speed +1, slam damage ×1.35, red/orange sprite tint
    — Separation force so golems spread and surround
  XP base: 45 per kill  (total up to 135 if killed fast)

STAGE 3 — Minotaur  [MINI-BOSS]
  Count:   1
  HP:      500
  Behavior:
    — Wanders until player enters 3.5× display range
    — Charge sequence: roar SFX → 1s windup (charge direction locked, telegraph circle) →
      charge at MINOTAUR_CHARGE_SPEED dealing 60 damage
    — Charge cooldown: 5 seconds after landing
    — At 50% HP: charge speed doubles (MINOTAUR_ENRAGE_SPEED)
  Drop:    Pendant (100%, see Pendant table above)
  XP base: 180

STAGE 4 — Skeletal Archers
  Count:   6 archers
  Size:    85% of display tile
  HP:      20
  Damage:  25 per arrow   Shoot CD: ~1.5s
  Behavior:
    — Flee when hero closes within 2.2× display range
    — Strafe perpendicular to player while in shooting range (circling behavior)
    — Periodically flip strafe direction so they orbit rather than stand still
  XP base: 10 per kill  (low; ranged, often left alive during kiting)

STAGE 5 — Orc Grunts
  Count:   4 orcs
  Size:    110% of display tile
  HP:      220
  Damage:  42 per hit   ATK CD: ~1.3s
  Behavior:
    — Slow base movement (designed to be kited at distance)
    — Separation force so they spread and flank
    — Enrage at 40% HP: burst charge (ORC_CHARGE_SPEED) toward player;
      deals full damage on contact, then enters cooldown
    — On enrage: battle-cry SFX plays, sprite turns red
  XP base: 55 per kill  (total up to 220 if killed fast)

STAGE 6 — Trolls
  Count:   2 trolls
  Size:    2× display tile (large)
  HP:      200
  Damage:  20 AoE
  Behavior:
    — Slow movement, mostly stationary at range
    — Rock Throw: targets ground under current hero position
        · Projectile travels and lands, triggering AoE blast on impact
        · AoE radius: 2.8× display size
        · Throw cooldown: 5 seconds
        · SFX: slam.wav on impact
    — On death: Brute Death SFX
  XP base: 70 per kill

STAGE 7 — Ghoul Pit
  Count:   15 initial + 8-ghoul second wave
  Size:    80% of display tile
  HP:      20
  Damage:  15 per hit   ATK CD: ~0.87s
  Behavior:
    — Fast movement, whole-room chase range (always swarm)
    — Tight separation so they converge as a mob without fully stacking
    — Second wave: triggers when ≤5 ghouls remain alive;
      8 new ghouls spawn and ghoul-intro SFX replays
    — Designed to be destroyed by AoE abilities
  SFX:     Stage-start intro · Ghoul attack on hit · Ghoul death
  XP base: 6 per kill (fast decay; 23 total kills → up to 138 if fast)

STAGE 8 — Spell Blade  [MINI-BOSS]
  Count:   1
  HP:      Copied from player (STR-based) at player level+1
  Behavior:
    — Spawns as hue-shifted copy of the player's class sprite (195° hue rotate → purple tint)
    — Stats mirror player at level+1: primary stat +3, others +1
    — Random extra skill point assigned to Q, W, or E
    — Uses same AI as a melee enemy but with the player's damage values
    — Uses same death SFX as the player's class
  Label:   "[ClassName] LvN" in purple above health bar
  Drop:    Pendant (100%, see Pendant table above)
  XP base: 230

STAGE 9 — Twin Guardians
  Count:   2 guardians
  Size:    Display tile wide, 1.5× tall (spear-wielding statues)
  HP:      380 each
  Damage:  60 per spear lunge
  Behavior:
    — Always chase, faster movement than typical melee enemies
    — Separation force so they approach from different angles and flank
    — Spear Lunge (directional attack):
        · Triggers when hero enters 2.5× display range
        · Windup: ~0.83s — direction locked at trigger; dashed red line grows
          toward player showing the lunge reach
        · Hit zone: narrow rectangle extending 4.2× display size forward
        · Flash: gold spear-flash drawn on lunge
        · Cooldown: ~2.2s after lunge
    — Enrage on partner death: 2× speed, ×1.4 damage, ×0.55 cooldown;
      sprite tints red, "ENRAGED" label appears, HP bar turns orange
  SFX:     Guardian Hit on attack · Guardian Death on kill
  XP base: 75 per kill  (total up to 150)

STAGE 10 — John Pork  [FINAL BOSS]
  Count:   1 → splits recursively
  HP:      500
  Behavior:
    — Powerful cleave melee attacks
    — On death: splits into 2 copies at ½ HP and ½ damage
    — Each copy splits again on death (2nd generation at ¼ original)
    — Killing all copies wins the game
  Win screen: Hero summary — stats, skills, talents, score (XP gained /
              damage dealt / damage taken)
  XP base: 150

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
AI BEHAVIOR NOTES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  All enemy movement uses normalized vectors (no 8-direction snapping → no stutter).
  Separation force applied per-type prevents enemies from piling into one tile.

  Per-type highlights:
    Goblins    Chase + separation; fast attackers
    Golems     Permanent chase; slam AoE; enrage at 40% HP
    Minotaur   Charge sequence with direction lock and sound telegraph
    Archers    Strafe perpendicular to hero while shooting; flee when close
    Orcs       Slow kitable base + enrage charge burst at low HP
    Trolls     Ranged AoE rock throw aimed at player ground position
    Ghouls     Whole-room chase, tight separation, 2-wave spawn
    Spell Blade Melee spellblade mini-boss with block and dash attacks
    Guardians  Directional spear lunge with windup telegraph; partner-death enrage


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
XP ECONOMY SUMMARY
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  XP needed to level: 100 per level (levels 1–10 require 900 total)

  Max available XP if all enemies killed instantly (no decay):
    Stage 1   6 × 14  =  84
    Stage 2   3 × 45  = 135
    Stage 3   1 × 180 = 180
    Stage 4   6 × 10  =  60
    Stage 5   4 × 55  = 220
    Stage 6   2 × 70  = 140
    Stage 7  23 × 6   = 138
    Stage 8   1 × 230 = 230
    Stage 9   2 × 75  = 150
    Stage 10  ~4 × 37  ≈ 150  (splits scale down)
    ─────────────────────────
    Total             ≈ 1,487

  With fast kills: ~1,400+ XP available vs 900 needed → healthy ~1.6× buffer.
  XP decay (halves every 45s) and Rest choices reduce this buffer,
  encouraging aggressive play and stage completion speed.

  Expected level progression (fast play, no rests):
    After Stage 1:  ~Lv 2
    After Stage 2:  ~Lv 3–4
    After Stage 3:  ~Lv 5
    After Stage 4:  ~Lv 5–6
    After Stage 5:  ~Lv 7
    After Stage 6:  ~Lv 8
    After Stage 7:  ~Lv 9
    After Stage 8:  ~Lv 10
    Stages 9–10:    Already max or near-max
